5. Damages categories
Select all categories to include in the model
▼
Core overtime damages always included
OT base rate damages (1.5×)
1.5 × Regular Rate × OT Hours Variable × SOL Workweeks. Standard high-case model.
OT FWW rate damages (0.5×)
0.5 × FWW Rate × OT Hours Variable × SOL Workweeks. Low-case model. Mutually exclusive with 1.5× for totals.
Incentive rate damages (0.5×)
0.5 × Incentive Rate × OT Hours Variable × SOL Workweeks. Added to both 1.5× and FWW totals.
Liquidated damages
FLSA liquidated damages (100%)
Doubles unpaid wages. Good faith defense may apply. Report separately.
New York liquidated damages (100%)
Automatic 100% of unpaid NY wages. No good faith defense.
Massachusetts treble damages (3×)
Mandatory 3× all MA wage violations. No good faith defense. Replaces FLSA liquids.
Colorado liquidated damages
Automatic: greater of $1,000 or 200% of wages. Willful: greater of $3,000 or 300%. Report both.
Interest
Pre-judgment interest (simple)
Principal × (Days from midpoint of year to extrapolation date / 365) × Rate. Calculated on OT and FWW models separately.
%
Break penalties CA / CO
Meal break premiums
SOL Workweeks × Base Rate × Missed Meals per Week. CA: 1 hr pay. CO: 0.5 hr pay per missed break.
Rest break premiums
SOL Workweeks × Base Rate × Missed Rest Breaks per Week. CA: 1 hr pay. CO: 1/6 hr pay per missed break.
Wage statement & notice penalties NY / CA
Wage statement penalties
NY: MIN($250 × workweeks × 5, $5,000). CA: $50 first pay period, $100 each subsequent, max $4,000.
Wage notice penalties
NY only: MIN($50 × workweeks × 5, $5,000) per employee.
Post-separation & waiting time CA
Post-separation waiting time penalty
CA only, terminated employees: Last Base Rate × 8 × 30.
PAGA CA only
PAGA penalties
Per-pay-period CA labor code penalties. Report separately from other CA damages; provide standalone and aggregate totals.
PAGA categories
Other damages case-specific
Minimum wage liquidated damages
State minimum wage × OT Hours Variable × SOL Workweeks.
RSU damages
Overtime underpayment from improper exclusion of RSU value from regular rate. Requires vesting records.
Tipped minimum wage / tip credit damages
(Min Wage – Cash Wage) × Hours. Used when tip credit is unlawfully applied.
NY spread of hours pay
Extra 1 hour at minimum wage for any workday where spread exceeds 10 hours. NY only.
Other
Any additional damage category not listed above.
