5. Damages categories
Select categories — notes available for caveats and adjustments
▼

  

    

      
      
Flag anything cutting across multiple categories — risk discounts, unusual facts, global adjustments.

      
    

    

      
Core overtime damages

      

        
OT base rate damages (1.5×)
1.5 × Regular Rate × OT Hours × SOL Workweeks. Standard high-case model.

        
OT FWW rate damages (0.5×)
0.5 × FWW Rate × OT Hours × SOL Workweeks. Low-case model. Mutually exclusive with 1.5× for totals.

        
Incentive rate damages (0.5×)
0.5 × Incentive Rate × OT Hours × SOL Workweeks. Added to both 1.5× and FWW totals.

      

    

    

      
Liquidated damages

      

        
FLSA liquidated damages (100%)
Doubles unpaid wages. Employer may avoid by proving good faith and reasonable belief of compliance.

        
New York liquidated damages (100%)
100% of unpaid NY wages. Good faith defense exists but burden is on employer and rarely succeeds in civil actions (post-2011).

        
Massachusetts treble damages (3×)
Mandatory 3× all MA wage violations. No good faith defense. Replaces, does not stack with, FLSA liquids.

        
Colorado liquidated damages
Automatic: greater of $1,000 or 200%. Willful: greater of $3,000 or 300%. Report both scenarios.

Select states where interest applies. Statutory rates are pre-filled — override if needed. Applied to unpaid wages only, not liquidated damages.

Break penalties CA / CO

      

        
Meal break premiums
SOL Workweeks × Base Rate × Missed Meals/Week. CA: 1 hr/missed break. CO: 0.5 hr/missed break.

        
Rest break premiums
SOL Workweeks × Base Rate × Missed Rests/Week. CA: 1 hr/missed break. CO: 1/6 hr/missed break.

      

    

    

      
Wage statement & notice penalties NY / CA

      

        
Wage statement penalties
NY: MIN($250 × workweeks × 5, $5,000). CA: $50 first period, $100 subsequent, max $4,000.

        
Wage notice penalties
NY only: MIN($50 × workweeks × 5, $5,000) per employee.

      

    

    

      
Post-separation & waiting time CA

      

        
Post-separation waiting time penalty
CA only, terminated employees: Last Base Rate × 8 × 30.

      

    

    

      
PAGA CA only

      

        

          
PAGA penalties
Per-pay-period CA labor code penalties. Report separately from other CA damages.

Other damages case-specific

      

        
Minimum wage liquidated damages
State min wage × OT Hours × SOL Workweeks.

        
RSU damages
Overtime underpayment from exclusion of RSU value from regular rate.

        
Tipped minimum wage / tip credit damages
(Min Wage – Cash Wage) × Hours. Used when tip credit is unlawfully applied.

        
NY spread of hours pay
Extra 1 hour at minimum wage for workdays where spread exceeds 10 hours. NY only.

        
Other
Any additional damage category not listed above.

      

    

  







  
6. Merit analyses
Exemption risk, time analyses, headcount breakouts
▼

  

    

      
7i merits analysis
Employees where incentive pay exceeds regular pay, yearly.

      
HCE merits analysis
Non-CA, non-NY employees above HCE threshold. Annualize partial years. CO has separate thresholds.

      
Proxy time / off-the-clock analysis
Time analysis to establish uncompensated work — login/logout data, badge records, call logs, email timestamps. Feeds into damages as merits basis for OT claims.

      
Severance — headcount & workweeks
Report headcount and workweeks for employees with severance agreements. Include in total workweeks; display separately on summary.

      
Arbitration — headcount & workweeks
Report headcount and workweeks for employees subject to arbitration. Include in total workweeks; display separately on summary.
